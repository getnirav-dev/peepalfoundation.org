Welcome to Peepal Foundation

At Peepal Foundation, we believe that true education doesn’t end at academics; it begins with empathy, awareness, and the courage to contribute. That’s why we focus on holistic education, the kind that nurtures not just the mind but the heart and character of every child.

Children from underserved communities often grow up without the support they need to discover their voice, build confidence, or even dream beyond survival. We exist to change that.

Since 2001, our vision has been clear: to make holistic education accessible to every child so they can grow into kind, responsible, and active citizens of this country. Through life skills, civic awareness, creative expression, and academic support, we’re shaping a generation of change-makers.

Because when children thrive, communities transform.
