Our Journey

WHEN YOU SUPPORT ONE CHILD, YOU CHANGE A FAMILY

WHEN YOU SUPPORT MANY, YU CHANGE THE FURURE OF A COMMUNITY

One Child’s Growth Inspires Many


At Peepal Foundation, we believe every child deserves more than survival; they deserve the chance to grow with dignity, to learn with curiosity, and to dream with courage.

  • Our journey began in 2001, in a modest setting with just 30 children. They came from homes and schools that couldn’t always support their learning or well-being.
  • Today, more than 35,000 stories have unfolded since. Stories of children who now walk into hospitals as nurses, into classrooms as teachers, into offices as engineers. Because someone believed in them when it mattered most.
  • At our Development Centre, we’ve helped over 5,600 children return to school and stay.
  • And through our flagship CEDE Program, we go beyond textbooks. We spend 80+ hours a year with children from grades 2 to 5, nurturing life skills that often go unspoken, like hygiene, empathy, civic sense, values, communication, and a love for art and culture.
  • We’ve worked with children across 60+ schools, planting seeds of self-worth and responsibility and helping them grow not just as students but as active and responsible citizens of our country.
  • We know change doesn’t happen overnight. But it begins with one child. One opportunity. One person who cares.
This is our story. And it continues to unfold with every child, every classroom, every heart that believes in a better tomorrow.
