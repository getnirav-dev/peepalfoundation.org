Our Leadgership

BREAKING THE CYCLE OF INEQUALITY

Guiding Change with Vision and Purpose


Peepal Foundation is guided by a passionate and visionary leadership committed to social transformation through education. Rooted in empathy, integrity, and action, our leaders inspire change at every level. Their unwavering dedication drives our mission to nurture socially responsible citizens.



Vineet Kharat


Vineet Kharat is a Pune based film maker and media professional who co-founded V R Interactive Solutions, a production house that specialises in corporate and documentary films, animation and virtual reality immersive storytelling. His commitment to projects that benefit children, youth, and the elderly dates back to his time at Fergusson College. Motivated by his desire to empower the poor through revolutionary projects, he established the Peepal Foundation in 2001. His dedication to social impact is demonstrated by his involvement with numerous renowned organizations, including Swayam Shikshan Prayog, WWF, SPIC MACAY, and Eklavya Nyasa. Vineet’s leadership extends to women’s empowerment, environmental care, and supporting marginalized communities. Through his diverse roles, he continues to shape positive change, guided by a deep sense of compassion and responsibility.



Dr. Trupti Mehta


Dr. Trupti Mehta is a Pune ‑based consummate healthcare professional with nearly 20 years of experience in all aspects of hospital management and administration. Dr. Trupti Mehta specialises in heading operations with key focus on bottom line profitability by ensuring optimal utilization of resources and streamlining the working SOPs for enhanced operational effectiveness. At Poona Hospital, where she has worked since 2007, she has Built a reputation for excellence in managing hospital planning, designing & development projects / initiatives for providing basic & high end medical facilities. She is an avid reader and an intrepid traveller but most of all she loves dogs.



Roopa Rampura


Roopa Rampura is a Pune-based graphic designer and media entrepreneur with a Master’s in Computer Management and a diploma in Graphic Art and Animation. She has a strong blend of artistic talent and technical expertise, having served as Production Head at Kirloskar Computer Services, where she led a team of 20 artists and animators. A skilled painter and graphic designer, Roopa is also deeply passionate about animal welfare, actively helping rehabilitate injured strays. As a co-founder of V R Interactive Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a leading production house in Pune, she has contributed to over 200 films as a creative director. In addition to her professional work, Roopa serves as the treasurer of the Peepal Foundation, supporting various community initiatives.



Ranjit Jadhavrao


Ranjit Jadhavrao is among the top language translators in Pune, having taken to language skills as a young boy watching his father work passionately on several translation jobs for a top publication. Ranjit is a voracious reader, and this adds a sharper edge to his work in translation. Ranjit’s rich experience includes his working spell in a leading corporate publishing house in Pune, having worked on a broad spectrum of assignments for over 21 years. Soon, he was bitten by the entrepreneurship bug and set up his own business in language translations, designing and printing. Ranjit is an avid trekker and loves exploring our beautiful country. He has been associated with the founders of Peepal Foundation for many years and enjoys sharing his time with Peepal-related projects.



Shailendra Yashwant


Shailendra Yashwant is an environmental photographer, writer and journalist. brings over a decade of experience in non-profit management, having worked with various organizations focused on climate change, sustainable development, and community empowerment. His extensive experience has equipped him with a unique understanding of the challenges and opportunities in the non-profit sector, allowing him to develop and implement effective strategies for positive impact. Throughout his career, Yashwant has held leadership positions in several organizations, driving initiatives and programs that have made a tangible difference in the lives of individuals and communities worldwide.



Shirish Kaulgud


Mr. Shirish Kaulgud, a reputed businessman and owner of a leading IT company in Pune is well-known for his promotion of Art and Culture across Maharashtra. He has been a key resource and guiding force at SPIC MACAY Pune for the last 2 decades. He is an honorary member of the Peepal Foundation since its inception.



Punam Dharkar


Punam Dharkar comes with vast experience in various fields of work. She has worked as a copywriter with various top advertising agencies in Mumbai. Later, in Pune, she was a visiting faculty in creative advertising sessions at Symbiosis Institute of Mass Communications. She also simultaneously took up a second career in leadership training for a few years, in an MNC. She is now involved as a founder member of a very successful group called Swap for pre-owned household items that you can buy, sell, exchange, lend, borrow and give away. She is a mentor with and well-wisher of Peepal Foundation, a mentor at Ishanya Foundation for guiding students who learn to develop skills that can make them job-ready. Besides, she is part of a scrap band called Band Baja De where lyrics are written to promote waste segregation, adhering to traffic safety rules, and cleanliness, which are sung to Bollywood tunes at traffic lights, bastis, and at related events. She also plays the role of the secretary of her housing society having worked on several projects for uplifting the ‘brand value’ of the society.
