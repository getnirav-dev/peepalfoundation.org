At Peepal Foundation, our journey is made stronger by the unwavering support of our valued partners. Their commitment, resources, and belief in our mission help us reach deeper and grow wider. Together, we make the vision of nurturing socially responsible citizens a living reality.

Our Supporters


We are deeply grateful to our esteemed corporate partners, including Ferrero India Pvt. Ltd., Cotiviti India Pvt. Ltd., Fronius, and Cilicant. Their trust, commitment, and generosity have played a pivotal role in expanding our reach and making a lasting impact in the communities we serve. Through their collaboration, we have been able to scale our efforts, creating opportunities for thousands of children to access quality education, a supportive learning environment, and the tools they need to succeed in life.
